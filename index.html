Mission Statement

The fraternity professes to create a bond of brotherhood passed down from generation to generation, including lasting relationships between alumni and currently active members.

Internally

Nu Alpha Phi emphasizes brotherhood and promotion of self-development for all of its members, as encouraged by its seven fundamental principles of Brotherhood, Discipline, Leadership, Pride, Professionalism, Service, and Unity. Members are taught to use these principles in everyday life to promote personal growth. These ideals are brought upon prospective members, as well.

Externally

Nu Alpha Phi seeks to promote Asian-awareness, as well as to serve as a liaison between the Greek community and the Asian community. This is done through a supportive network of alumni and matriculated brothers who actively engage in participation in community service, cultural workshops and events, university programs, and various other on-campus student activities.

MGC Fusion

NYU MGC Dance Event!

The Multicultural Greek Council (MGC) at New York University proudly presents the annual Step & Stroll Showcase! The MGC focuses on unifying and creating opportunities for all of its member organizations. Through its actions the MGC promotes the fraternity and sorority ideals of service to our communities, leadership, culture, and brotherhood/sisterhood. The MGC also expresses the uniqueness of Multicultural Greek Life. Our council consists of our amazing 12 sororities and fraternities.

Rush Spring 2018!

Join the tightest brotherhood!

Rush is a period during the school year for students to attend a variety of events who may be interested in being a part of Greek life. Through these events held during Rush, you can learn more about our fraternity as well as to get to know our Brothers on a personal level. Rush is open to anyone from any background. We encourage you to come out to these events as this is a chance for us to get to know you on a personal level as well. All events hosted by us are FREE and non-obligatory.
